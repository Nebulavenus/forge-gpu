Acknowledge the user's statement. Do not take action.

Usage: /dev-fyi <statement>

## Rules

- Read and understand what the user said
- Save to memory if the information is useful for future conversations
- Respond with a brief acknowledgement (one sentence max)
- Do not start any work
- Do not stop any ongoing work
- Do not make suggestions
- Do not ask follow-up questions
- Do not make changes to any files (except memory)
- Do not interpret the statement as a request
